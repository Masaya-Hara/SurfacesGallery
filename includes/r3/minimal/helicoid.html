Helicoid

Weierstrass data is $(g, \omega) = (e^z, ie^{-z} dz)$. This is a ruled surface generated by a straight line that moves in a helical path aroud a fixed axis, which is introduced by Leonhard Euler in 1744 and Jean Baptiste MEusnier in 1776. See also Catenoid.
