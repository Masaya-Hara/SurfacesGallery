Minimal surfaces

Minimal surfaces in $\mathbb{R}^3$ can be locally given as $$ f = \int^z \bigl( 1-g^2,\: i(1+g^2),\: 2g \bigr) \omega,$$ where $g$ is a meromorphic function and $\omega$ is a holomorphic 1-form such that $g^2 \omega$ is holomorphic.
