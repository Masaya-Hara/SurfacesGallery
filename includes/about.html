Purpose of This Site

As mentioned in Others as well, there already exist many galleries and repositories of surfaces in differential geometry, and the examples I can provide represent only a small portion, with many overlapping cases. However, I believe that having multiple websites publish repositories of surfaces from different perspectives can invigorate each field and would by no means have a negative impact. With due respect to the existing galleries and repositories, I hope to contribute to an environment where any student and researcher can more easily access a broader range of data.

Available Resources

This website serves as a repository for the surfaces I have created to date. If you find it troublesome to generate surfaces yourself, or are simply interested in exploring them without creating your own, this site is ideal for you!

You can browse through the available surfaces and click “View in 3D” to launch the interactive viewer. For every surface presented here, the source data is also available via GitHub.
You will find thumbnail .jpg images, .obj and .mtl files for the 3D viewer, and when available, accompanying Mathematica files. (The Mathematica files are expected to be compatible with version 12.2.0.0 or later.)

Contact
