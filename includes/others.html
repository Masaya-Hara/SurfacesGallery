Other Galleries

Currently, there exist many galleries and repositories related to surfaces (or curves) considered in differential geometry. Here, I would like to introduce—albeit without formal permission—some of the websites that I have frequently referred to in my own work.

  • Prof. Sisto Baldo,  Gallery of Minimal Surfaces
  • Prof. Robert Ferréol,  MATHCURVE.COM
  • Prof. Shoichi Fujimori,  Minimal Surface Gallery
  • Prof. Mario B. Schulz,  Geometric Analysis Gallery
  • Prof. Nich Schmitt,  Geometrie Werkstatt
  • Prof. Nich Schmitt,  The GANG Gallery of Constant Mean Curvature Surfaces
  • Prof. Matthias Weber,  Minimal Surface Repository
