Gallery of Surfaces

Explore geometric surfaces interactively in 3D space

This website serves as a repository for the surfaces I have created to date.

If you find it troublesome to generate surfaces yourself, or are simply interested in exploring them without creating your own, this site is ideal for you.

You can browse through the available surfaces and click “View in 3D” to launch the interactive viewer.

For every surface presented here, the source data is also available via GitHub.
You will find thumbnail .jpg images, .obj and .mtl files for the 3D viewer, and when available, accompanying Mathematica files.

The Mathematica files are expected to be compatible with version 12.2.0.0 or later.
